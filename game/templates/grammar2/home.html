Welcome to Hippo and Parakeet: Pull and Rush, a new perspective on the concepts of parataxis and hypotaxis.

Practice these concepts by analyzing extracts from literature books. You will then face three stages:

1. Classification: telling if it is complex or simple.

2. Boundaries: separating the clauses by adding ‘//’ for simple clauses or ‘///’ for complex clauses.

3. Labeling: categorizing the clauses according to the way they relate to each other.

Once you have finished all the extracts, you will know your final score. The person who has the most points at the end of the fair wins a special prize (if there are two with the most points, the first one to get them wins)

But before starting, let’s meet Parakeet and Hippo, two good friends who ended up racing in their taxis. All for the sake of explaining to you these tricky concepts in a funny way, so do not let them down!
